P2: Event

Idea 1: Recipe Building, Website
An invitation is sent to those who submitted an ingredient to participate in recipe building on the website. Using PHP, the recipes would be automatically uploaded to the website as they are submitted. Everyone who submits a recipe is given a physical cookbook one compiled.

+++ Can span a few days, everyone can submit on their own time
--- Not a communal event, gathering / posting images will be hard
??? Ability to see / use submissions as they come

Idea 2: Recipe Building, E-mail
An invitation is sent to those who submitted an ingredient to e-mail back a recipe. Everyone who submits a recipe is given a physical cookbook one compiled.

+++ Can span a few days, everyone can submit on their own time, can e-mail pictures easily
--- Not a communal event, can't see what's been submitted
??? Anonymous until the book comes out, maybe the surprise is nice, chance of similar or duplicate recipes, how two people make the same dish

Idea 3: Recipe Building, Virtual Event
An invitation is sent to join a Zoom. Together, we talk informally about the ingredients, and people talk about recipes they'd make, everyone typing them into the chat.Everyone who submits a recipe is given a physical cookbook one compiled.

+++ Adds a face to a name, ingredient; more commual, social, communicative
--- Time zones, scheduling, high chance of low-attendance, Zoom fatigue
??? Live event has possibility for chance
